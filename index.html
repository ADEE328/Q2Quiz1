Popular Filipino Dishes

Dish Description

Adobo

 A savory stew of meat marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, and spices, known as the unofficial national dish of the Philippines.

Sinigang

 A sour soup made with tamarind and various meats or seafood, often served with vegetables.

Lechon

 Whole roasted pig, known for its crispy skin and tender meat, typically served at celebrations.

Kare-Kare

 A rich and savory stew made with oxtail, tripe, and vegetables in a peanut sauce, served with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste).

Sisig

 A sizzling dish made from chopped pig's head and liver, seasoned with calamansi, chili, and spices.

Bicol Express

 A spicy stew made with pork, coconut milk, chili peppers, and shrimp paste, originating from the Bicol region.
